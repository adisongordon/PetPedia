Volunteer at Ruff Haven!

Volunteering at Ruff Haven is a rewarding way to make a difference for pets and their families in need. Whether you’re a Cat Room Volunteer, a Dog Walking Volunteer, or a Delivery Driver for our Pet Pantry, there are many ways to get involved and support our mission.

Basic requirements for volunteers:

  • You must be at least 18 years old.
  • Have a working email and Internet access to sign up for shifts.
  • Be able to purchase a $10 volunteer t-shirt (waived if needed) and wear it during each shift.
  • Commit to at least one 2-hour shift per month for a minimum of six months.

By volunteering, you’re helping provide comfort and care for pets during challenging times and contributing to a community that values compassion and support.
